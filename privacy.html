Privacy Policy

Overview

This privacy policy explains how the Ollama Email Assistant handles your data and protects your privacy while using our Chrome extension.

Data Collection and Processing

The Ollama Email Assistant is designed with privacy-first principles:

  • All email processing occurs locally through your Ollama instance
  • No email content or personal data is transmitted to external servers
  • No user tracking or analytics are implemented
  • No cookies or local storage are used to store personal information

Local Processing

Here's how your data stays secure:

  • Email content remains within your local environment
  • AI processing happens exclusively on your machine
  • No cloud services or external APIs are involved
  • Direct connection only to your local Ollama instance (localhost)

Technical Implementation

Security measures in place:

  • Communication restricted to localhost (127.0.0.1)
  • No external network requests for processing
  • Memory-only processing - no persistent storage
  • Automatic data clearing after each session

Your Rights

As a user, you have complete control:

  • Full ownership of all processed data
  • No data retention by the extension
  • Freedom to disable or uninstall at any time
  • Direct access to all processing through your Ollama instance

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices. Users will be notified of any significant changes through the extension or this website.
